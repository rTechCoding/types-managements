# types-managements
This repository provides a clear and structured overview of different types of management used in organizations, businesses, and projects. It is designed as a learning and reference resource for students, professionals, and beginners in management studies.

Strategic Management

Operational Management
Manages day-to-day operations to ensure efficiency and productivity.

Human Resource Management (HRM)
Deals with recruitment, training, performance, and employee relations.

Financial Management
Handles budgeting, investment decisions, financial planning, and cost control.

Marketing Management
Focuses on market research, branding, promotion, and customer engagement.

Project Management
Plans, executes, and delivers projects within scope, time, and budget.

Quality Management
Ensures products and services meet defined quality standards.

Risk Management
Identifies, analyzes, and mitigates business risks.

Supply Chain Management
Manages the flow of goods, services, and information from source to customer.
